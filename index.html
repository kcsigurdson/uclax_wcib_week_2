Rumours was released on February 4th 1977 as the eleventh studio album by the British-American band. The album features both pop rock and soft rock influences which included the use of the electric keyboard. Reaching the top of both the US Billboard 200 and the UK Albums Chart, Rumours was praised for the quality of the production and harmonies and went on to win Album of the Year in 1978. Since its release the album has sold over 40 million copies and is still one of the best-selling albums of all time.
